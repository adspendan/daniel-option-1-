      FAQ

      How fast can we start?

      Discovery call → audit → install. Initial ads typically launch in 5–10 business days depending on creative readiness and Operator scope.

      What platforms do you run?

      Meta (FB/IG), TikTok, YouTube/Google; email/SMS where it lifts ROAS. OperatorHQ integrates across platforms for unified actions and reporting.

      Is the AI actually private?

      Yes. Operators run local/on‑prem on a Mac Studio with our proprietary application. We use local LLMs via Ollama where appropriate and the latest high‑performing models (e.g., OpenAI gpt‑oss‑20B) when needed. Your data stays in your environment.

      What hardware does it run on?

      A Mac Studio (M4). We can provide it or install on your device/VPC. Deployed on‑site or privately in your cloud.

      Do you handle HIPAA?

      HIPAA‑compliant deployment options are available for medical workflows. Data is processed locally with audit trails and access controls.

      What does maintenance cost?

      Monthly maintenance ranges from $500–$5,000 depending on architecture and number of automations. Includes updates, monitoring, support, and new automation rollouts.

      Do I need ads to use the Operator?

      You can run the Operator without ads, but the best results come from combining direct‑response campaigns with automation. We do both.
